Activity Page

This is our Activites page:

At our Club there are many different activities you can participate in, from Competitions, which involves different clubs from around the county participating in swimming races against each other to see which club has the best talent and also freindly races that are arranged by us where people race for small prizes and deals on memberships, this is just for members in the club. To 1 to 1 sessions and group sessions, which are part of the membership but some are not covered. Our Club ranges in abilities from professionals all the way to first time swimmers. We treat every member of our club with the same courtesy and support aswell as having a welcoming and willing to help staff.

1 to 1 sessions:

"

1 to 1 sessions are for people that dont care about competing in competitions and they just want to improve themselves or to get more healthy and fit. For the 1 to 1 sessions we have lots of personal trainers that will advice you on diet, excerise and give you goals to reach and an end goal that you want to get to. 1 to 1 sessions can be booked/purchased from our website or you can go into the club.

Group sessions:

"

Group sessions are mainly used for fitness and are a more cheaper option than 1 to 1 sessions. Also in group sessions we put on more than just swimming sessions. We have aqua aerobics, weights under water and lifeguard training. Group sessions can be booked/purchased from our website or you can go into the club.
