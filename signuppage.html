About us

Our swimming club (The Flying Fishers) was founded in ancient parts of Egypt. The clubs competitive status is currently Professional.

There are many unique techniques within the Flying Fishers Club, including the Atlas bear paddle and the Himalayan brown bear stroke.

Specialities

Our club does not always teach well known strokes and instead we specialise in creating our own strokes. Some strokes can be dangerous whilst others can be very tiring. The strokes require inhuman reactions which means members have to act like fish in order to swim like fish, hence the clubs name.
