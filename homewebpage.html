Welcome to MMSL.Ltd

MMSL.Ltd swimming club is a popular and well loved worldwide, people of every ages go to one of our many sites around the world, our original club being made in egypt.

A lot of novice and pro swimmers come and use some of our clubs resources, olympic swimmers sometimes coming to certain club areas and helping with the group sessions, we also are able to bring in these olympic athletes to judge our swimming competitions making this whole experience for our users more enjoyable and a real once in a lifeime experience.

Though our website you will be able to learn a bit more about the company along with what our club offers in the sense of equiptment, facilities and different events that we host. Check out the Activity's page to see most of these.
